The Amazing Rabbit Man

Along with moving fast and camouflague, Rabbit Man's main super power
is the ability to hear someone in dager from miles away. He's actually the
super hero that does all the grunt work for Spider Man and Dare Devil.
He saves the city because it's his city where he is respected and loved.
