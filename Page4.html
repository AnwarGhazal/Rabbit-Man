Why is Rabbit Man so hard to spot in NYC?

Everyone knows where he's been by the graffitti and beautiful works of art
you see all over town.The thing is he's so fast, you won't even see him coming,
like art ninja. He has an amazing ability to camouflague into his surroundings,
which make him nearly impossible to see. He moves fast weather walking
or throwing up graffitti on the wall, he works like he's on fast forward.
